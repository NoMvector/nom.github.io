Khmer new year
(Khmer New Year; Khmer: បុណ្យចូលឆ្នាំខ្មែរ ), also known as Choul Chnam Thmey (Khmer: ចូលឆ្នាំថ្មី; enter the New Year) and Moha Sangkranta (Khmer: មហាសង្ក្រាន្ត) or just Sangkranta, is the traditional celebration of the solar new year in Cambodia.
Credit : Veasna GD

Mid-Autumn Festival
in China, Mid-Autumn Festival is a celebration of the rice harvest and many fruits. Ceremonies are held both to give thanks for the harvest and to encourage the harvest-giving light to return again in the coming year.
